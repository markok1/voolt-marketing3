FAQs

What if I already have a website?

Even if you already have a website, you can still benefit from Voolt Marketing. We offer a convenient option where you supply your existing website, answer a few questions, and we'll automatically set up your campaigns on Google and Facebook [Meta].

How does your pricing work?

With Voolt Marketing, you only pay for the advertising budget that you set. You can choose your budget from different tiers, toggle auto-renewal on or off, and adjust it at any time. We set up your campaign, and for each click you receive on Google or Meta, we deduct the cost from your Voolt account balance. If your balance starts to get low, we'll notify you to top it up yourself or, if you've enabled auto-renewal, we'll do it for you.

Do I need to perform any manual tasks if I don't want to?

No, you don't! Voolt Marketing is designed to be fully automated, allowing you to focus on your business while our platform takes care of the technical aspects. Our automated processes handle your ads, optimization, learnings, and our system can manage your leads as well.

Once you've provided information about your business and location, Voolt's algorithms and AI technology get to work. They operate behind the scenes to generate and optimize your ads for Google and Facebook (Meta), and automate marketing tasks. This means you can sit back and relax, secure in the knowledge that Voolt is actively supporting your online presence.

How will my ads appear?

After you set your budget, we’ll find the best spots on Facebook (Meta) and at the top of Google search, in the sponsored section, to showcase your brand to a local audience seeking professionals like you.

Can I track website visitors and traffic?

Absolutely! Our platform offers built-in analytics that provide valuable insights, including visitor numbers, page views, and popular content. This allows you to easily understand the effectiveness of your online presence and make data-driven decisions to optimize your website and tailor your marketing efforts.

How will I receive my new leads?

When potential clients click on your ads, they'll be redirected to your website. If they fill out a form there, you'll instantly receive this lead in your Voolt account. This information will be available in your dashboard, and you'll receive a notification via email and SMS. Also, if potential customers call you, we'll record the conversation, making it available in your dashboard.

Can I insert old customers on my leads page manually and manage all of them together?

Of course! You can add their information directly to your dashboard and manage them all in one place.

How does call recording with clients work?

Whenever a client calls your number, we’ll record the call and make it available to you. This way, you can easily keep important information they may have shared with you or listen to their messages if you didn’t answer their call. We’ll also register their number, so you can easily call them back.

Do you offer 24/7 Customer Service?

Yes! Our dedicated support team is available round-the-clock to address your inquiries and provide guidance.

Whether you have technical questions, need help with website customization, or require assistance with any aspect of our platform, you can reach out to our customer service team through various channels, including live chat and email. We are always here to assist you.
